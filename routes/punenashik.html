Travel comfortably from Pune to Nashik with reliable cabs, experienced drivers and flexible pickup options.

Pune to Nashik is a popular intercity route in Maharashtra, covering approximately 210 km. The journey takes around 4–5 hours depending on traffic and road conditions.

Nashik is famous for its temples, vineyards, spiritual importance and scenic surroundings, making it a perfect destination for pilgrims, families and leisure travelers.

Best Places to Visit in Nashik

Trimbakeshwar Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva.

Sula Vineyards

Famous wine estate offering tours and tastings.

Panchavati

Historic area linked with Ramayana legends.

Ramkund

Sacred bathing ghat on the banks of Godavari river.

Anjaneri Hills

Birthplace of Lord Hanuman with trekking trails.

Coin Museum

Unique museum showcasing ancient Indian currency.

Best Places to Eat in Nashik

Sula Restaurants

Fine dining with vineyard views and global cuisine.

Hotel Panchavati

Popular spot for traditional Maharashtrian food.

Barbeque Ville

Perfect place for buffet lovers and families.
