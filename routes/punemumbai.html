Comfortable, safe and affordable cab service from Pune to Mumbai with experienced drivers and multiple vehicle options.

Pune to Mumbai is one of the most frequently traveled routes in Maharashtra. The distance is approximately 150 km and the journey takes around 3–4 hours via the Mumbai–Pune Expressway. VeloTaxi ensures a smooth ride with well-maintained vehicles and professional drivers.

Best Places to Visit in Mumbai

Gateway of India

Iconic monument overlooking the Arabian Sea and a must-visit landmark.

Marine Drive

Beautiful sea-facing road also known as the Queen’s Necklace.

Elephanta Caves

UNESCO heritage caves famous for ancient rock-cut sculptures.

Juhu Beach

Popular beach for sunsets, street food and evening walks.

Siddhivinayak Temple

One of the most famous and revered temples in Mumbai.

Bandra–Worli Sea Link

Architectural marvel connecting Bandra and Worli.

Top Places to Eat in Mumbai

Leopold Cafe

Iconic cafe near Colaba known for multi-cuisine food and historic charm.

Bademiya

Famous late-night street food spot for kebabs and rolls.

Britannia & Co.

Legendary Irani cafe serving Parsi dishes like Berry Pulao.

Juhu Beach Street Food

Must-try pav bhaji, bhel puri and sev puri by the sea.

The Bombay Canteen

Modern Indian restaurant celebrating regional Indian flavors.

Trishna

Renowned seafood restaurant famous for butter garlic crab.
