Pune to Lonavala Cab Service

Enjoy a short, scenic and refreshing journey from Pune to Lonavala with comfortable cabs, expert drivers and flexible booking options.

Pune to Lonavala is one of the most popular weekend routes in Maharashtra. The distance is approximately 65 km and the journey takes around 1.5–2 hours via the Mumbai–Pune Expressway.

Lonavala is famous for its misty hills, waterfalls, viewpoints and peaceful environment, making it a perfect getaway for families, couples and groups.

Best Places to Visit in Lonavala

Lion’s Point

Popular viewpoint offering breathtaking valley views.

Bhushi Dam

Monsoon hotspot with waterfalls and shallow water pools.

Rajmachi Fort

Historic fort with trekking trails and scenic views.

Karla Caves

Ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves with stunning architecture.

Tiger’s Leap

Cliff-top viewpoint offering panoramic valley scenes.

Wax Museum

Fun attraction featuring lifelike wax statues.

Best Places to Eat in Lonavala

German Bakery

Popular café for snacks, desserts and quick bites.

Rama Krishna

Famous for South Indian and vegetarian meals.

Chikki Shops

Must-visit for Lonavala’s famous chikki and fudge.
