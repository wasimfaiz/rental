Pune to Goa Cab Service

Enjoy a comfortable and scenic road journey from Pune to Goa with professional drivers, clean vehicles and flexible cab options.

Pune to Goa is one of the most scenic long-distance road trips in India. The distance is approximately 450 km and the journey takes around 9–11 hours depending on the route and traffic.

Popular routes include Pune–Kolhapur–Amboli Ghat and Pune–Belgaum highway, offering lush greenery, ghats and beautiful landscapes. VeloTaxi ensures a safe, relaxed and enjoyable ride with experienced outstation drivers.

Best Places to Visit in Goa

Baga Beach

Famous for nightlife, water sports and vibrant beach shacks.

Calangute Beach

Known as the Queen of Beaches, perfect for relaxation.

Dudhsagar Waterfalls

One of India’s tallest waterfalls surrounded by forests.

Anjuna Beach

Popular for flea markets and sunset views.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

UNESCO World Heritage church in Old Goa.

Fort Aguada

Historic fort with panoramic sea views.
