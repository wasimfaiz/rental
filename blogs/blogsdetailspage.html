Top 5 Tips for Safe Cab Travel

Jan 10, 2025 VeloTaxi Team 4 min read

Cab travel has become an essential part of daily commuting and outstation journeys. To ensure a safe and stress-free experience, it’s important to follow some basic safety practices.

1. Verify Driver & Vehicle Details

Always check the driver name, vehicle number and photo before starting your trip.

2. Share Trip Details

Share your trip status with family or friends for added safety.

3. Choose Trusted Cab Services

Book from reliable cab providers like VeloTaxi for professional drivers and verified vehicles.

4. Sit in the Back Seat

This provides better safety and comfort during the ride.

5. Stay Alert

Keep track of your route and stay aware throughout the journey.
